how to get component address:
1) Get the transaction-payload-hash by querying the transaction status with your transaction id: 
https://betanet-gateway.redoc.ly/#operation/TransactionStatus

2) Use the transaction-payload-hash to query the commited details: 
https://betanet-gateway.redoc.ly/#operation/TransactionCommittedDetails 

3) Find the component address at the end of the response
